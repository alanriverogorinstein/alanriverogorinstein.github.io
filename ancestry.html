My work for Ancestry

Ancestry

It is my pleasure and immense luck to contribute to the work of a brilliant institution that brings much joy and a feeling of profound belonging to million of people every day: Ancestry. In my role as a designer of their mobile app I think deeply about how to make the experience of going on a journey of personal discovery beautiful, easier, and more rewarding.

Avatar builder app

 Why even bother

One of the most useful ways to represent an ancestor on your family tree is using a little illustration. It's useful to have an avatar in there, and one of my colleagues, Anna, drew a collection of beautiful avatars that can be customized. My intention to further this idea and defend it so that it gets built, I wrote an app that lets users feel what it would feel like to use Anna's beautiful custom avatars by making a custom avatar.
